Welcome To Domki Letniskowe Zygmunt - comfort, peace and wildlife in a chalet at an affordable price!

Domki Letniskowe Zygmunt is a cozy resort in the heart of Kopalino, North Poland. Nearby there are sheltered sandy beaches, great days out for the kids, cycling, beautiful gardens and nurseries. Young or old, there is something for everyone.

Our cottages offer a cozy environment for big families or groups of friends to enjoy the untouched nature of the north beaches in Poland!

Surroundings

Restaurants

In the vicinity of the cottages, you can find the two restaurants Bar Nina and Bar Lesly. A great place to spend evenings filled with joy with friends or family accompanied by a cozy atmosphere and appetizing traditional polish food and much more.

Forests

We can say that the magic of any place is largely maintained by the nature that surounds it. Kopolino is no exception, bringing to the attention of anyone who visits it the unforgettable landscapes. It is said that forests are the Earth's lungs but in contrast, they can take people's breath away with their astonishing views.
