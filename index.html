Welcome To Domki Letniskowe Zygmunt - comfort, peace and wildlife at an affordable price!

Domki Letniskowe Zygmunt is a cozy resort in the heart of Kopalino, North Poland. Nearby there are sheltered sandy beaches, great days out for the kids, cycling, beautiful gardens and nurseries. Young or old, there is something for everyone.

Our cottages offer a cozy environment for big families or groups of friends to enjoy the untouched nature of the north beaches in Poland!
